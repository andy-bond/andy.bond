## Review

Overall, _The Lost City_ was a surprisingly entertaining watch - although I'll admit I didn't have super high expectations going in.

_Channing Tatum_ is essentially the same character he played in _21 Jump Street_, but it's hard not to love watching him play a loveable idiot.

My only criticism of the movie is that I would have liked to see more of _Daniel Radcliffe_ playing the villain - the scenes he was in were well done and I enjoyed watching him play a maniac hell-bent on getting his hands on a crown.
