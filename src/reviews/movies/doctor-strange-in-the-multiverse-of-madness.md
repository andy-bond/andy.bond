## Review

Overall, this was a solid story with great performances across the board. This won't be in the top Marvel movies of all time list, but that doesn't mean it wasn't a great film!

I expected to have way more cameo appearances but, honestly, was pleasantly surprised with the references & surprises that this movie showed. At least one surprise was one that I had hoping for!

I enjoyed the character of America Chavez quite a bit - I hope she returns in future films. I am somewhat disappointed in the direction they left Wanda and Dr. Strange, but I am intrigued as well.
